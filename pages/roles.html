Roles, Ownership & Daily Workflow

How Jackie and Devesh split ownership on the Ten Thousand account, and what to check every morning.

Role Split

Jackie — Creative & Content Strategy

Primary strengths: content planning, creative strategy, audience development, brand voice.

Owns:
  • Ad creative pipeline — briefing, reviewing, rotating creatives
  • Demand Gen audience strategy — runner vs. hybrid segments, app/event signals
  • Content calendar coordination with Patrick's team (new product launches, editorial moments)
  • Creative performance reads — which hooks, formats, and angles are working
  • UGC / horizontal video sourcing and creative adaptations
  • Snap creative testing and format experimentation
  • Meta ramp-up creative plan (when it comes back online)

Devesh — Analytics, Optimization & Campaign Math

Primary strengths: data analysis, bid/budget strategy, pacing, knowing when to pull levers.

Owns:
  • Daily pCPA and ROMS monitoring — flag issues before they become client questions
  • Budget pacing — are we on track for monthly spend/revenue goals?
  • Bid strategy adjustments — when to raise/lower tROAS, shift budgets between campaigns
  • Campaign restructuring decisions — when a campaign should be paused, split, or consolidated
  • Shopping feed performance tracking — is the supplemental feed moving the needle?
  • Negative keyword maintenance and search term audits (especially PMax)
  • Evergreen vs. Experimental spend containment — make sure experimental doesn't creep past 15%
  • Weekly performance narrative for client calls — "here's what happened, here's why, here's what we did"
Shared responsibility: Both Jackie and Devesh should be in the weekly client call. Jackie leads on creative updates and content calendar alignment. Devesh leads on performance narrative, pacing, and optimization updates. Balaji oversees strategy and steps in on big-picture items (feed, account restructure, new channel decisions).

Daily Check-In Workflow

What to look at every morning (10 min)
# Check Who What You're Looking For
1 Yesterday's pCPA Devesh Is it above $65? If so, what drove it — was it a Snap spike, a bad Google day, or a new customer dip? Single bad days happen. Two consecutive bad days = investigate.
2 Budget pacing Devesh Check the budget deviation heatmap. Any campaign >20% over or under daily target? Over-delivery on a low-ROAS campaign is money wasted. Under-delivery on a winner is opportunity cost.
3 Experimental spend % Devesh Is Snap (or any experimental) creeping above 10-15% of total spend? If yes, pull back before it inflates pCPA enough to trigger client questions.
4 Creative delivery Jackie Any ad groups with creative fatigue (frequency >3, CTR declining WoW)? Flag for rotation. Any new creative launched that needs early reads?
5 New product / calendar check Jackie Anything launching this week from Patrick's calendar? New product = update supplemental feed + brief creative + align campaign targeting.
