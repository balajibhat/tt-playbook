Weekly Rhythm: Day-by-Day

Client call is Tuesday. Internal sync is Monday. Everything else flows around those two anchors.

Monday — Internal Sync & Week Setup Prep day
Time Task Who
AM Devesh: Pull weekend + last week numbers. Check pCPA, ROMS, budget pacing, any weekend spend spikes (especially Snap). Flag anything off-track.
Jackie: Run the 4-bucket creative triage on all active ads. Classify each as Winner, Engagement Driver, Underperformer, or Fatiguing. 		Devesh + Jackie
Midday Internal sync (30 min): Balaji + Jackie + Devesh. Review:
• Weekly pacing table — are we on track?
• Evergreen vs. Experimental pCPA — any containment issues?
• Creative triage results — what's scaling, what's dying, what needs rotation?
• Anything unusual to flag for Tuesday client call?
• Assign who covers what on the client call 		All three
PM Devesh: Kill Underperformer ads, adjust budgets on Fatiguing Creative, set up graduation ad sets for test winners. Update campaign tagging if needed.
Jackie: Pause fatigued creative, queue new variations. Check Patrick's content calendar for this week. 		Devesh + Jackie
Tuesday — Client Call Day Game day
Time Task Who
AM
Pre-call		 Devesh (30 min): Final data pull. Update weekly pacing with Monday's numbers. Prepare performance narrative — 2-3 bullet points per campaign bucket. Have pCPA breakdown ready.
Jackie (15 min): Prepare creative + content update. New creative launched? Demand Gen reads? UGC status?
Together (15 min): Run through the 9-item pre-call checklist. No surprises. 		Devesh + Jackie
Call Devesh leads: Performance narrative, pacing, pCPA story, budget updates, optimization actions taken.
Jackie leads: Creative updates, Demand Gen audience insights, content calendar alignment, asset requests.
Balaji: Strategy, big-picture items, feed updates, new channel decisions, relationship. 		All three + client
PM
Post-call		 Jackie: Send follow-up — action items, assets requested, next steps. Log creative briefs.
Devesh: Implement optimization decisions from the call. Update budget allocations. Log action items. 		Jackie + Devesh
Wednesday — Optimization & Creative Production Focus work
Time Task Who
AM Devesh: Search term audit (Google). Add negatives. Check PMax search term insights. Review budget deviation heatmap.
Jackie: Creative production — write new ad copy, brief new visual assets, coordinate with Patrick's team. 		Devesh + Jackie
PM Devesh: Bid strategy review. Are tROAS targets still appropriate? Any campaigns stuck in learning? Check Snap pCPA 7-day trend.
Jackie: Demand Gen audience performance review — Runners vs. Hybrid. Creative-level insights. 		Devesh + Jackie
Thursday — Testing & Launches Launch day
Time Task Who
AM Jackie: Launch new creative — test ads, variations of Fatiguing Creative, assets from Patrick's team. QA all ads before launch (copy, links, tracking, mobile preview).
Devesh: Set up new test campaigns/ad sets. Configure graduation ad sets. Ensure tracking is firing. 		Jackie + Devesh
PM Both: Monitor new launches — are they getting delivery? Any disapprovals? Early CTR reads reasonable? Don't optimize yet — just verify things are running. Both
Friday — Weekly Close & Planning Wrap up
Time Task Who
AM Devesh: Mid-week check — anything off-track since Monday? Budget adjustments before the weekend? Set weekend budget caps (especially Snap).
Jackie: Update creative roadmap / icebox. What concepts are in the pipeline for next week? Upcoming product launches or calendar moments? 		Devesh + Jackie
PM Both: Light touch. Set up automated rules for weekend monitoring. Ensure nothing is going to blow up. Log open items for Monday's internal sync. Both
Weekend — Minimal Touch Automated monitoring
Devesh: One quick check Saturday morning (5 min). Are spend levels normal? Any dramatic CPA spikes? If Snap or any experimental channel is overspending, pause or reduce immediately. Otherwise, don't touch anything.

Jackie: Off unless a creative emergency (ad disapproval, broken link flagged by automated rule).

Automated rules should catch: CPA >200% of target → pause ad set. Budget >150% of daily target → alert. Disapproved ads → email notification.
The rhythm in one sentence: Monday = sync + triage. Tuesday = client call (prepped). Wednesday = optimize + produce. Thursday = launch + test. Friday = close + plan. Weekend = hands off.
